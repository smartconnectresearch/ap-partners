Partner Questions Answered

How is this different from typical referral programmes?

Most programmes pay once. We pay for 12 months on ALL client work - initial projects, expansions, add-ons, and renewals. One good introduction can generate £15-20k versus a typical £500-2000 referral fee.

What types of clients are ideal for SCR?

High-stakes, insight-driven organisations with complex, multi-market research needs. This includes: global research agencies (Ipsos, Kantar), management consultancies (EY, Accenture), and corporates in healthcare, tech, and financial services. They value precision execution, operational control, and partners who protect their reputation. Minimum project: £50k.

What size projects generate £15-20k in commissions?

Here's the breakdown: For £15k total commissions, the client needs to spend approximately £200k over 12 months (£50k initial project at 10% = £5k, £120k in months 1-6 at 5% = £6k, £160k in months 7-12 at 2.5% = £4k). For £20k commissions, approximately £300k spend. Most SCR clients spend £150-300k in year one, making these earnings very achievable.

What's your close rate on partner referrals?

Our warm referrals from partners typically close at 60-70% because they come pre-qualified and with trust already established. We only accept referrals that match our ideal client profile (high-stakes organisations needing complex multi-market research), ensuring higher success rates for everyone.

Do we need to change how we operate?

Not at all. Simply refer research opportunities you encounter during your normal business activities. We handle everything else - sales, delivery, invoicing, support.

When do commissions get paid?

Within 30 days of client payment. New clients typically sign within 60 days, RFPs within 28 days. You could see first commissions within 30-45 days of introduction.

How do I register as a partner?

This is an exclusive, invitation-only programme with limited partners per category (translation agencies, lead gen firms, tech partners, fieldwork & panel partners). To secure your place: email us with your company details. If approved, you'll receive partner agreement and onboarding within 48 hours. We're accepting 2-3 quality partners per category - once we reach capacity, applications close.

Is there any exclusivity requirement?

No exclusivity required. Work with whoever you choose. However, most partners naturally send more opportunities our way once they see the revenue potential and client satisfaction.
